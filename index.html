About Me

There is a famous quote "Every great developer you know got there by solving problems they were unqualified to solve until they actually did it." and I aspire to be the one. To start with I never thought I would like to write code, But I took a leap and Started my professional journey with Larsen and Toubro Infotech. To my surprise I never looked back. Me being a person who cannot live with monotony , my carrier choice gave me the kick I wanted to be on my toes and learn new things infinitely.

I worked as a full stack developer for 5 years. I worked on web application for an insurance company. I got to work on huge variety of stack including Java, Javacript, Node.js, React, ExtJs , Sql , mongo db . I tried my hand on everything. As I continued to work , I learnt that anyone who knows the logic can write the code with all the help available on internet , but Its difficult to master it. Its easier to make changes to already built system but its difficult to build one from scratch. Knowing a language is different than mastering it. That's what triggered my masters journey. Masters introduced me to different carrier choices like data engineer, AI/ML developer and researcher. I am looking forward to try different trajectories.

Apart from my professional , Me as a person is very enthusiastic to know new people. Listening is the key to understand a person. I love to host house parties and also to cook on weekends for friends and family. I like visiting serene naturesque places. I like to read and watch content related to mystery genres. I am not involved in sport much nowadays , yet I have completed my black belt in taekwondo (form of karate).
